Future Bio:

Nayvadius DeMun Wilburn, better known as "Future", is an American rapper born and raised from Atlanta, Georgia. From 2011 to 2014, he rose to prominence with albums Pluto and Honest.

Other known albums include DS2, The Wizrd, and HNDRXX. Now, some of his most well known songs include Mask Off, Where Ya At, Life is Good, and Low Life. He has collaborated with many well known artists such as Young Thug, Drake, Travis Scott, and Lil Uzi Vert.
